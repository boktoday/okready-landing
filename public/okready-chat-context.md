## The Antifragile Mindset
The core philosophy: antifragility is not about surviving the storm — it's about building a ship that gets stronger the more it sails through rough water. Three categories: Brittle (broken by shock), Robust (unharmed by shock), Antifragile (strengthened by shock).

### Seven Principles
1. Embrace Small Stressors — managed exposure to stress builds strength
2. Build Redundancy — two kidneys, two lungs, two income streams
3. Use Via Negativa — remove fragility before adding strength
4. Favour Decentralisation — local over centralised
5. Practice Optionality — keep options open
6. Learn Through Skin in the Game — real stakes produce real learning
7. Respect the Nonlinear — small changes produce massive effects
