# Pillar 01 — Mindset & Systems

## CO-STAR System Prompt

Copy this prompt into your preferred AI tool (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, offline LLM, etc.). It will generate a personalised Week 1 action plan based on your situation.

---

```
CONTEXT:
I'm a beginner building personal resilience and self-sufficiency in Australia. Below is my current situation:

- Location: [YOUR_SUBURB], [YOUR_STATE]
- Dwelling: [HOUSE/APARTMENT/UNIT — BEDROOMS — YARD/NO_YARD]
- Household: [LIST_EVERYONE — AGES — ANY_SPECIAL_NEEDS]
- Existing supplies: [WHAT_YOU_ALREADY_HAVE — e.g. first aid kit, torch, canned food]
- Budget: $[AMOUNT] per week for preparedness
- Primary concern: [COST_OF_LIVING / SUPPLY_CHAINS / NATURAL_DISASTER / HEALTH / OTHER]
- Time available: [HOURS] per week
- Experience level: [COMPLETE_BEGINNER / SOME_KNOWLEDGE / EXPERIENCED]
- Key constraints: [E.G. RENTING — CAN'T_MODIFY_WALLS / LIMITED_STORAGE / MOBILITY_ISSUES]

OBJECTIVE:
Generate my personalised Week 1 action plan for the "Mindset & Systems" pillar. Start with the absolute foundations: risk assessment and a basic emergency preparedness baseline. The plan must account for the specific people in my household — standard generic prepper advice won't work for us.

STYLE:
Direct, practical, encouraging. No jargon. No fear-mongering. Every action should include a clear "why" in one sentence.

TONE:
Calm and confident. Like a knowledgeable friend who's done this before and understands family complexity.

AUDIENCE:
An Australian beginner. Not a prepper — just someone who wants their family to be OK when things get tight.

RESPONSE FORMAT:
- Week 1 Goal (one sentence summarising what I'll achieve)
- Priority Action #1 — [TIME_ESTIMATE] (quickest win, highest impact)
- Priority Action #2 — [TIME_ESTIMATE]
- Priority Action #3 — [TIME_ESTIMATE] (deepest, only if time allows)
- Shopping List for this week (items + approximate cost, totalling under my weekly budget)
- Adaptations for my household (specific to the people and constraints I listed)
- One "win" to aim for by end of week (what success looks like)
- 2-sentence why-this-matters for this week
```

---

## What to expect

After running this prompt, the AI will produce a customised Week 1 plan that accounts for your location, household, budget, and constraints. Save the output and work through it. Next week, you can run the prompt again with updated context (what you completed, what changed) and it will generate Week 2.
