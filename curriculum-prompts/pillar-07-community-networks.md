# Pillar 07 — Community Networks

## Onboarding Questionnaire

Answer these before running the prompt.

---

1. **Location:** What suburb and state? How long have you lived there?
2. **Dwelling type:** House, apartment, or unit?
3. **Current community connection:** Know no neighbours, know a few, or active in the community?
4. **Local knowledge:** None, know local amenities, know local risks, or well connected?
5. **Skills I can offer:** What could you teach or help a neighbour with? (organising, admin, childcare, gardening, tech, cooking, trades, etc.)
6. **Skills I'd like to learn:** What would you love a neighbour to teach you?
7. **Communication options:** Phone, WhatsApp, ham radio, CB radio, satellite messenger, or none?
8. **Transport:** Car, public transport, bicycle, or none?
9. **Household type:** Single, couple, family, or shared? Any isolation concerns?
10. **Personality note:** Introverted, social, or new to the area?
11. **Completed Pillar 01:** Not started / completed Week 1 / completed?

---

## CO-STAR System Prompt

Copy this into your AI tool and replace bracketed sections with your answers.

---

```
CONTEXT:
I'm building community network resilience. Based on my onboarding questionnaire:

- Location: [ANSWER 1]
- Dwelling: [ANSWER 2]
- Current community connection: [ANSWER 3]
- Local knowledge: [ANSWER 4]
- Skills I can offer: [ANSWER 5]
- Skills I'd like to learn: [ANSWER 6]
- Communication: [ANSWER 7]
- Transport: [ANSWER 8]
- Household: [ANSWER 9]
- Personality: [ANSWER 10]
- Completed Pillar 01: [ANSWER 11]

OBJECTIVE:
Generate my personal Week 1 action plan for "Community Networks." Start with low-risk, low-pressure actions. If I'm introverted or new to the area, give me scripts for what to say. The goal is one real connection this week — not a full neighbourhood network. Map local assets first, then identify one connection opportunity.

STYLE:
Practical, socially-aware. Assume social anxiety where stated. Provide actual scripts and conversation starters.

TONE:
Warm and encouraging — community building is vulnerable work. Like a friend who's also introverted but has figured out how to connect.

AUDIENCE:
An Australian who may be isolated, new to their area, or just doesn't know where to start with neighbours.

RESPONSE FORMAT:
- Week 1 Goal (one sentence — must be achievable if you're introverted)
- Local asset map — 3 things to find within 1km
- Connection strategy — one low-friction way to meet a neighbour
- Script — what to actually say (fill-in-the-blank)
- What I can offer — how to position my skills as useful to neighbours
- One community-anchored activity (community garden, street library, Buy Nothing group)
- One activity to do with my kids/partner that builds community
- Follow-up for Week 2 — what comes next if Week 1 works
```
